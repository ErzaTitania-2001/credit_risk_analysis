EXAMPLE PROMPTS FOR CREDIT RISK AGENT

=== Member-Based Analysis (Database Lookup) ===

Low Risk Examples:
- "Analyze credit risk for member 63044350"
- "What's the risk assessment for member 63044350?"
- "Evaluate loan application for member 65190234"
- "Get credit profile for member -29890"

High Risk Examples:
- "Evaluate loan application for member -76653"
- "Analyze member -76653"
- "What's the credit risk for member -34711?"

General Member Queries:
- "Look up member 105"
- "Retrieve borrower data for member 63044350"
- "Show me information for member -76653"

=== Direct Calculations (No Database Lookup) ===

Risk Score Calculations:
- "Calculate credit risk for FICO 600 and income $45,000"
- "What's the risk for someone with FICO 750 and income $80,000?"
- "Assess risk for FICO 500 and annual income $30,000"
- "Calculate risk score for FICO 700 and income $65,000"
- "What's the risk for FICO 650 and income $55,000?"

Expected Credit Loss Only:
- "Calculate expected credit loss for a $20,000 loan with risk score 35"
- "What's the ECL for a $15,000 loan with risk score 23.53?"
- "Calculate ECL for $10,000 loan and risk score 50"
- "Expected loss on $25,000 loan with 45 risk score"

=== Complete Risk Assessments (Multi-Step) ===

Full Analysis:
- "I need a complete risk assessment: FICO is 650, income is $55,000, and loan amount is $15,000"
- "Full analysis for FICO 700, income $70,000, loan $20,000"
- "Complete assessment: FICO 600, income $45,000, requesting $12,000 loan"
- "Analyze borrower with FICO 750, income $80,000, seeking $18,000"

Scenario Comparisons:
- "Compare risk for FICO 600 vs FICO 700 with same income of $50,000"
- "What's the difference in ECL for $15,000 vs $20,000 loan at risk score 30?"
- "How does income of $50,000 vs $80,000 affect risk for FICO 650?"

=== Edge Cases and Boundary Tests ===

Boundary Values:
- "Calculate risk for minimum FICO 300 and income $1"
- "What's the risk for maximum FICO 850 and income $1,000,000?"
- "Assess risk score exactly 30 with $10,000 loan"
- "ECL for risk score exactly 60 with $15,000 loan"

Zero/Minimal Values:
- "Calculate risk for FICO 650 and zero income"
- "What's ECL for $0 loan with risk score 50?"
- "Risk for FICO 300 and income $100"

High Values:
- "Calculate risk for FICO 850 and income $500,000"
- "ECL for $1,000,000 loan with risk score 5"

=== Error/Invalid Prompts (For Testing) ===

Non-Existent Members:
- "Analyze credit risk for member 999999999"
- "Look up member 12345678"
- "Evaluate member 0"

Invalid FICO:
- "Calculate risk for FICO 200 and income $50,000"
- "What's risk for FICO 900 and income $60,000?"
- "Risk for FICO -100 and income $40,000"

Invalid Income:
- "Calculate risk for FICO 700 and income -$50,000"
- "Risk for FICO 650 and negative income"

Missing Information:
- "Calculate credit risk for annual income $50,000" (missing FICO)
- "What's the risk for FICO score 700?" (missing income)
- "Calculate risk for a borrower" (missing all parameters)

Vague Requests:
- "Tell me about credit"
- "What is risk?"
- "Help me with loans"

=== Conversational/Natural Prompts ===

- "I'm applying for a $15,000 loan. My FICO is 680 and I make $62,000 per year. What are my chances?"
- "Can you check if member 63044350 is a good candidate for approval?"
- "My credit score is 620 and my income is $48,000. Should I be concerned about default risk?"
- "What would be the expected loss on a $20,000 loan if the risk is considered medium?"
- "Is FICO 300 really bad? What does that mean for loan approval?"
- "Compare the risk between member 63044350 and member -76653"

=== Follow-Up Questions ===

After Risk Calculation:
- "What if the income was $10,000 higher?"
- "How much would the ECL change with a $5,000 larger loan?"
- "What FICO score would I need to get into low risk?"
- "Explain why this is considered high risk"

After Member Lookup:
- "Calculate their expected credit loss"
- "What's their risk category?"
- "Is this borrower approvable?"
- "Compare this to member 63044350"

=== Use Case Specific ===

Loan Officer:
- "Quick assessment for walk-in customer: FICO 690, income $58,000, wants $12,000"
- "Batch check these members: 63044350, -76653, 65190234"

Risk Manager:
- "What's our exposure on a $50,000 loan portfolio with average risk score 35?"
- "Calculate ECL for worst-case scenario: $30,000 loan, risk score 70"

Underwriter:
- "Member -76653 was previously declined. What was their risk score?"
- "Verify if member 63044350 qualifies for premium rate"

Portfolio Analyst:
- "What percentage of our borrowers are in high risk category?"
- "Average ECL for medium risk borrowers?"
