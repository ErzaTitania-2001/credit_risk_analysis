CREDIT RISK DATABASE INFORMATION

Dataset: Lending Club Loans (2007-2018)
Source: Kaggle - 2 million+ loan records
Sample Size: 200 borrowers

Risk Distribution:
- Low Risk (<30): 140 borrowers (70%)
- Medium Risk (30-60): 40 borrowers (20%)
- High Risk (>60): 20 borrowers (10%)

FICO Score Range: 300-850
- 300: Worst possible credit
- 850: Perfect credit
- Typical range: 600-750

Sample Member IDs:
- 63044350: Low risk (FICO 779, Income $96,000, Loan $15,000, Status: Current)
- -76653: High risk (FICO 300, Income $69,558, Loan $17,000, Status: Declined)
- -29890: Excellent (FICO 850, Income $500,000, Loan $3,500)
- 65190234: Low risk (FICO 744, Income $150,000, Loan $14,400)

Risk Calculation Formula:
Base Score = 100 - (FICO / 8.5)
If Income > $80,000: Subtract 15 points
Final Risk Score = Between 0 and 100

Expected Credit Loss (ECL):
ECL = Loan Amount × (Risk Score / 100)

Examples:
- FICO 779, Income $96k → Risk Score: 0.00 (Low)
- FICO 650, Income $55k → Risk Score: 23.53 (Low)
- FICO 600, Income $45k → Risk Score: 29.41 (Low/Borderline)
- FICO 300, Income $70k → Risk Score: 64.71 (High)

Data Source:
Real loan data from Lending Club via Kaggle's "Lending Club Loan Data" dataset containing accepted and rejected loan applications from 2007-2018.
